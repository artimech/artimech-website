2. Analysis of the Core Operational Challenge

Handling over 20,000 disputes monthly is a testament to Presolv360's market leadership, but it also exposes a critical vulnerability. The current manual process is not just a workflow issue; it is a strategic limiter on growth, profitability, and service quality.

Operational Drag

The need to manually process diverse inputs (emails, scans with OCR, images, multi-lingual text) consumes thousands of hours, directly increasing operational cost per case and creating significant backlogs (e.g., the current 2,600+ pending awards).

Scalability Ceiling

Onboarding new enterprise clients and higher case volumes is constrained by the linear scalability of the legal team. True growth requires decoupling case volume from manual effort, which the current system prevents.

We will build an AI Co-pilot designed to augment, not replace, your expert arbitrators. The system automates the repetitive, time-consuming tasks, freeing up arbitrators to focus on high-value work requiring human judgment.

7. Data Security & Compliance

Security is paramount. The entire solution is designed with a security-first approach to protect sensitive case data and comply with all regulatory requirements, including RBI guidelines.

  • On-Premise/VPC Deployment: All training and deployment will occur within Presolv360's secure cloud environment. Sensitive data never leaves your control.
  • Data Anonymization: A dedicated pre-processing step will be implemented to anonymize Personally Identifiable Information (PII) before training.
  • Containerized Security: The AI model will be deployed in a hardened Docker container, isolating it from other systems and minimizing the attack surface.
  • Indian Data Sovereignty: All infrastructure will be located within India to ensure full compliance with regulatory mandates.
